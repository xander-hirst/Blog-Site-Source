Tournement Schedule

As some of you may know, the DotA Pro Circuit 2019-2020 has moved towards a Major and Minor tournement system throughout the year on the leadup towards The International 10

Each participating team may add and remove players if they wish up until the final Major, in which case the team rosters will be locked

Each tournment's top finishers will receive DPC points depending on how high they place, which are used to determine which teams will automatically qualify for The International

The top 12 teams with the highest DPC points will receive direct invites to The International 10

Mouse over specific tournaments to see the prizepool breakdown for each placing
