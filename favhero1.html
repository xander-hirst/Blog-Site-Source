Xander's Favorite Hero: Lone Druid

Lone Druid is an agility hero that is usually played in the offlane as a position 3, though it is recently being played position 1 in the safelane

He is a very unique hero due to his spirit bear ability, where Lone Druid controls a bear unit that scales with Lone Druid's level

He is my favorite hero for a lot of reasons

  • Lone Druid is one of the few offlaners that can consistantly contest the enemy safelaner and support while he is solo
  • There are a wide variety of effective item builds depending on how well you do in your lane
  • It is easy to get solo kills in the beginning of the game, allowing you to snowball
  • Microing the bear takes practice but it very rewarding once you get the hang of it
  • Towards the later stages of the game, so long as you used your ult and are constantly attacking with both the bear and the hero, you are basically invincable

My ability build

You always want to max Summon Spirit Bear first because it gives the bear a lot of HP and damage early game

Getting an early point in Savage Roar is useful and often will save your life as you try to run to safety when you get ganked

My item build

If my lane is not going very well or if our team needs to win early game, then I will skip radiance and instead build Mask of Madness into Maelstrom
